About Our HIV Testing Services

Nemba Hospital offers confidential and compassionate HIV testing services to individuals seeking to know their HIV status. Our testing facility is equipped with state-of-the-art technology and staffed by trained professionals who provide accurate and reliable testing results.

We offer various testing options, including rapid HIV testing and traditional blood tests, to accommodate different preferences and needs. Our goal is to make HIV testing accessible, convenient, and stigma-free for everyone.

At Nemba Hospital, we understand the importance of early detection and prevention in the fight against HIV/AIDS. That's why we also provide counseling and support services to help individuals understand their test results and access appropriate care and resources.
